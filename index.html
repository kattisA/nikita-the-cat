Neva Masquerade

The Neva Masquerade is a type of semi-longhaired domestic cat from Russia. It is closely related to the Siberian cat and is considered its colourpoint version. Its distinct markings come from a gene originally found in Asian cats. The breed likely came from crossing Siberian cats with Asian colourpointed cats or similar breeds like the Himalayan, Siamese, or Thai. The colourpoint Neva Masquerade has striking blue eyes and darker tabby markings on its face, ears, paws, and tail.
